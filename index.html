For Likamva

Likamva, from the moment I met you,
Something in me changed—something I never knew.
Your smile, so warm, like the sun at dawn,
Made me realize where my heart belongs.

I've tried to hide it, tried to be strong,
But the truth is, I've loved you all along.
Every laugh we’ve shared, every moment near,
Has only made my feelings clear.

You are more than just beautiful—you are light,
A presence that makes everything feel right.
And no matter how much I try to pretend,
I can’t deny what I feel in the end.

So here it is, my heart in these lines,
No more hesitations, no more signs.
Likamva, I don’t just like you—I love you, it's true,
And all I want… is a chance with you.
