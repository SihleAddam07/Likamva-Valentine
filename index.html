For You, Likamva

From the moment I saw you, something felt right,
Like a star in my sky, shining so bright.
Your laughter’s a melody, your presence a song,
With you by my side, I know I belong.

That night at the Matric Dance, you took my breath away,
Every smile, every dance, I wished time would stay.
The way you laughed, the way you shone,
Made the night the best I've ever known.

Every call we share, every word we say,
Brings joy to my heart in the sweetest way.
Your voice, your thoughts, the way you just *are*,
Feels like home, no matter how far.

So here’s my heart, open and true,
Asking if I can be more than a friend to you. ❤️
